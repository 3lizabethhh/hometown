About

Mesa is a desert city (check out the tumbleweed above) located in North America. More specifically, Mesa is contributes to the population of the USA with 327.2 million people as of 2018.Mesa is the third-largest city in Arizona USA with a population of 508,958 as of 2018. Mesa has 138.83 square miles of city and it's land is generally flat.The population density is 3,686/square mile. Mesa has a hot desert climate with mild winters with an average temperature low of 4 degrees celsus and very hot summers with an average high of 41 degrees celsius. Mesa's demographic is 83.8% caucasian, 27.4% hispanic/latino and the remaining precentage is a mix of asian,native american,pacific islander and a mix of two or more races. Mesa's major industry is their aeronautical industries that range from manufacturing to educational. They produce aeronautical equipment,military equipment,vehicles, vehicle safety systems and host a number of aviation training businesses. Mesa's is active in the tourism industry. Places to see includes the Mesa Arizona Temple and the Hohokam Baseball Stadium.

Cuisine

Three popular foods in Mesa:

The Burrito

One of the more common Mexican dishes in America, a burrito is a flour tortilla filled with a protein (usually chicken, pork, beef, or steak), rice, beans, and other fillings like lettuce and salsa before being wrapped so it's easy to hold in your hand.

The Sonoran Hotdog

It consists of a hot dog that is wrapped in bacon and grilled, served on a bolillo-style hot dog bun, and topped with pinto beans, onions, tomatoes, and a variety of additional condiments, often including mayonnaise, mustard, and jalapeño salsa.

The Taco

It is a traditional Mexican dish consisting of a small hand-sized corn or wheat tortilla topped with a filling. The tortilla is then folded around the filling and eaten by hand.
